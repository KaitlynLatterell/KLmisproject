Hobbies & Interests

Markets

Whether it is a flea, farmers', fall, or Christmas market, visiting markets is one of my favorite weekend activities. I love exploring local produce, handmade goods, and enjoying the vibrant atmosphere. It's a great way to support local farmers and artisans while discovering fresh, fun, and new ingredients for my cooking. Being able to interact with vendors and learn about their products adds a personal touch to the experience.

Outdoors

Exploring the outdoors allows me to connect with nature and find peace away from the hustle and bustle of daily life. Whether it's fishing, hiking, or simply enjoying a walk in the park, I cherish the beauty of the natural world and the tranquility it brings. I love boat rides, and spending my days outside both in the summer and winter.

Hiking

Hiking allows me to immerse myself in nature and experience the great outdoors. I love exploring new trails, breathing in the fresh air, and enjoying the stunning views from the top. It's a fantastic way to stay active and connect with the natural world. I love the reward that comes at the end of each hike: a breathtaking view that makes the effort worthwhile. Some of my faovrite places to hike so far have been Duluth, MN, Winona, MN, and Arenal, CR.
